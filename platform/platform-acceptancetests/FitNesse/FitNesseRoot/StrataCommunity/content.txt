A player can appear to another player as one of the below:
  * Not friend
  * Friend
  * Blocked
  * Blocked friend
  * Hidden
  * Hidden friend

Actions available to players are as below:
  * Add friend - send an invitation to become friend
  * Remove friend
  * Block 
  * Unblock
  * Private chat

Things to do with invitations: 
  * Reject friend - reject an invitation
  * Accept friend - accept an invitation and become friends
